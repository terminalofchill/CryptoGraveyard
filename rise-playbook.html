5 Mistakes That Kill Launches

üö´ Avoid These At All Costs

  • Launching without a real project: The community can smell vaporware instantly. Have something working before you launch, even if it's a prototype or MVP. "Coming soon" doesn't cut it.
  • Going dark after launch: Communication is everything. Post updates weekly at minimum. Silence kills momentum faster than bad news. Even "still working on X" is better than nothing.
  • Overpromising: Don't claim you're building "the next OpenAI." Be specific about what you're making and realistic about timelines. Underpromise, overdeliver.
  • Ignoring your community: These people are literally invested in your success. Answer questions. Take feedback seriously. Make them feel heard. They're your earliest supporters.
  • Selling too early: If you dump your tokens immediately after launch, trust evaporates instantly. You're in this for the long term ‚Äî act like it. Your credibility is your most valuable asset.
